About



My name is Saul Greene. I graduated from Washington State Universtiy in 2015 with a degree in Political Science (PreLaw) and continued onto law school at Colifornia Western School of law. I quickly decided that law school was not for me. I took some time to work and figure out what I wanted to do for the next chapter of my life. I loved the structure and locigal aspects of law school, however the legal field wants very in the box thinkers and lacks room for true creativity.


My cousin had taken courses with Code Fellows and suggested I apply. I was hesitant because I didnt like math for the most part, but after only a few days I fell in love. Programing combined the rule based structures and the creativity I sought. I learned that the logic i loved from law school was prevalent in programming and that there were so many ways to solve one problem in programming which fostered my creative desire.


Though I have only been programming for a short while, it is something I am truly passionate about. I never knew how to tell if you loved something until i stumbled upon programming. My 11 hour days feel like 3 hour days and even the stressful days are fulfilling.
